The Protoss capital ship, a mobile aircraft carrier that launches up to 8 Interceptors to devastate both ground and air targets. Each Interceptor deals 6 damage per hit, and with 8 Interceptors firing rapidly, a fully loaded Carrier unleashes extraordinary sustained DPS. Carriers are expensive (350/250 plus 200 minerals for Interceptors) and slow to build, but when massed, they become one of the most powerful forces in Brood War.

## Competitive Usage

Carriers are most prominent in PvT late-game, where Carrier-Arbiter compositions threaten to overwhelm Terran ground forces. In PvZ, Carriers were the traditional late-game Protoss answer before the Corsair-Reaver revolution, and they still appear in ultra-late-game scenarios. In PvP, Carriers are rare but can appear in drawn-out games. The critical threshold is reaching 4-6 Carriers — below that, they are vulnerable, but above it, they become extremely difficult to stop.

## Key Mechanics

Interceptors deal 6 Normal damage each and are launched sequentially from the Carrier. A fully loaded Carrier effectively attacks with 8 simultaneous sources of damage. Interceptors can be shot down — killing Interceptors degrades a Carrier's damage output without destroying the Carrier itself. Each replacement Interceptor costs 25 minerals and build time. Carriers have 4 base armor and 8 range, giving them excellent survivability and reach.

## Micro Techniques

- **Interceptor leashing**: Moving Carriers backward while Interceptors engage, keeping the Carrier body safe from anti-air
- **Focus fire rotation**: Targeting one enemy unit at a time with all Carriers to kill it before it can be repaired or healed
- **Interceptor reloading**: Building new Interceptors between engagements to maintain maximum firepower
- **Kiting**: Exploiting the Carrier's range and Interceptor mechanics to deal damage while retreating

## Counters

Massed Goliaths with Charon Boosters are the primary counter, outranging Carriers and dealing heavy Explosive damage. Scourge can rapidly destroy Interceptors or target the Carrier itself. EMP strips shields (150) from Carriers. Corsairs counter Interceptors efficiently with splash. Without Arbiter support, Carriers are vulnerable to focused anti-air.

## Historical Notes

The Carrier was the backbone of PvZ before Bisu's Corsair revolution. Players like Stork and Jangbi built their PvZ styles around Carrier-based late games. While less dominant today, the Carrier remains one of the most visually spectacular and thematically satisfying units in Brood War. Seeing a Carrier fleet in action — Interceptors swarming out in waves — is one of the game's iconic sights.
