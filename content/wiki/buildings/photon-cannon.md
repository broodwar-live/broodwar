The Photon Cannon is Protoss's static defense structure, attacking both ground and air units with 20 normal damage. It also functions as a detector, revealing cloaked and burrowed units within its detection range. The Photon Cannon requires a Forge before it can be built and must be placed within a Pylon's power field. Losing the Pylon powering a Photon Cannon disables it entirely.

In competitive play, Photon Cannons serve two distinct roles: defensive base protection and aggressive cannon rush strategies. Defensively, cannons at the natural expansion are the cornerstone of PvZ Forge Fast Expand builds, holding off Zergling pressure while the economy develops. Cannons at expansions protect against drop play and run-bys. The cannon rush is one of the most iconic aggressive strategies in Brood War — building a Pylon and Forge proxy near the enemy base, then warping in Photon Cannons to siege their mineral line or production.

Photon Cannon placement depends entirely on Pylon positioning since they require power. In defensive setups, cannons should overlap their attack ranges to create kill zones at choke points. At expansions, cannons behind mineral lines protect workers from harassment. The detection radius makes cannons valuable even in low numbers against cloaked threats like Dark Templar or burrowed Lurkers. With only 100 HP and 100 shields, individual cannons are relatively fragile, so strategic placement and numbers matter more than raw durability.
