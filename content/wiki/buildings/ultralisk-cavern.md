The Ultralisk Cavern is a late-game Zerg tech building that enables Ultralisk production from the Hatchery. It researches two upgrades specific to Ultralisks: Chitinous Plating (+2 base armor, stacking with Carapace upgrades for extremely high damage reduction) and Anabolic Synthesis (increased Ultralisk movement speed). The Ultralisk Cavern requires a Hive, placing it at the highest tier of the Zerg tech tree.

In competitive play, the Ultralisk Cavern unlocks Zerg's premier heavy ground unit. Ultralisks are massive, high-HP melee units with splash damage that excel at absorbing fire and dealing area damage to clustered ground forces. They are most effective when paired with Defilers — Dark Swarm makes Ultralisks immune to ranged attacks, allowing them to close distance and devastate enemy formations with their Kaiser Blades. The Ultralisk-Defiler combination is one of the most powerful late-game army compositions in ZvT, often forcing Terran into desperate responses.

The Ultralisk Cavern must be built on creep and requires a Hive. It is typically built at the main base alongside the Defiler Mound during the Hive tech transition. Both Chitinous Plating and Anabolic Synthesis should be researched before committing to Ultralisk production, as un-upgraded Ultralisks are slow and lack the armor to justify their high cost (200 minerals, 200 gas, 6 supply). The Ultralisk Cavern is a significant gas investment on top of the Hive cost, so it is usually built only when the Zerg player has secured enough gas income from multiple expansions to sustain Ultralisk production.
