The Defiler Mound is a late-game Zerg tech building that enables Defiler production from the Hatchery. It researches two critical abilities: Consume (sacrifice a friendly Zerg unit to restore 50 Defiler energy) and Plague (deals 300 damage over time to all units in an area, reducing them to a minimum of 1 HP). Defilers also have the innate Dark Swarm ability, which creates a cloud that makes units inside immune to ranged attacks. The Defiler Mound requires a Hive.

In competitive play, the Defiler Mound unlocks what many consider the most strategically impactful late-game unit in Brood War. Defilers with Dark Swarm fundamentally change ZvT — casting Dark Swarm over advancing Zerglings and Ultralisks renders Terran Marines and Siege Tanks unable to damage them with ranged fire, forcing Terran into melee engagements they cannot win. Plague is devastating against clumped armies, reducing unit HP across an entire formation. Consume research is essential since Defilers need energy to cast, and sacrificing Zerglings (cheapest Zerg unit) is the standard method.

The Defiler Mound must be built on creep and requires a Hive, placing it firmly in the late-game tech tree. It is typically built at the main base. Getting the Defiler Mound and completing Consume research as quickly as possible after Hive is a key timing for Zerg's transition to late-game army compositions. In ZvT, the arrival of Defilers with Dark Swarm often marks the turning point where Zerg's ground army becomes nearly unstoppable against bio-mech compositions. Protecting the Defiler Mound is critical during the Hive transition period.
