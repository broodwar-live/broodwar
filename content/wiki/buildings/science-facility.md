The Science Facility is Terran's advanced technology building, enabling Science Vessel production at the Starport and researching Irradiate and EMP Shockwave. It supports two mutually exclusive add-ons: the Physics Lab, which unlocks Battlecruiser production and Yamato Cannon research, and Covert Ops, which unlocks Ghost production (along with Academy), Lockdown, and Personnel Cloaking research.

In competitive play, the Science Facility is a mid-to-late game building primarily built for Science Vessel access. Science Vessels are essential in TvZ for Irradiate (devastating against Mutalisks and Overlords) and detection against Lurkers. EMP Shockwave is critical in TvP to drain Protoss shields and High Templar energy before engagements. The Physics Lab and Covert Ops add-ons see less frequent use — Battlecruiser builds are rare in professional play, though Ghost-based strategies with Lockdown appear occasionally in TvP.

The Science Facility does not lift off. Placement should account for add-on space on the right side if a Physics Lab or Covert Ops is planned. Since the Science Facility is typically built later in the game when the base is already established, it is usually placed safely behind production buildings. Losing a Science Facility is a significant setback, as it delays critical Irradiate or EMP research that enables Terran's mid-to-late game army composition.
