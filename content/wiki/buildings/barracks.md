The Barracks is the first military production building available to Terran and the gateway to all infantry units. It produces Marines by default, with Firebats and Medics unlocked once an Academy is built, and Ghosts available after an Academy and Covert Ops are completed. The Barracks is a prerequisite for the Factory, making it essential for every Terran build order regardless of strategy.

In competitive play, the Barracks defines Terran's early game. Wall-offs using a Barracks and Supply Depot at the natural ramp are standard in TvZ to prevent Zergling run-bys. Proxy Barracks — built near the enemy base — enable aggressive Marine or Marine-Bunker rush strategies that can end games early. Bio-focused builds (Marine, Medic, Firebat compositions) revolve around multiple Barracks for sustained production, with 5-Barracks or more common in mid-to-late game TvZ.

The Barracks can lift off and relocate, which is useful for scouting, repositioning wall-offs, or swapping add-on buildings with other structures. Placement near the ramp for wall-offs is the most common positioning, but players sometimes place Barracks deeper in the base to protect them from early aggression. In TvT and TvP, early Barracks placement and the number of Barracks built signal the Terran player's strategic intentions to the opponent.
