The Starport is Terran's air unit production facility, producing Wraiths, Dropships, Valkyries (requiring an Armory), Science Vessels (requiring a Science Facility), and Battlecruisers (requiring a Science Facility with Physics Lab). The Control Tower add-on enables Cloaking Field research for Wraiths and is required for producing certain advanced units. The Starport is a prerequisite for the Science Facility.

In competitive play, the Starport's most important roles are Dropship production for drop harass and Science Vessel production for detection and Irradiate. In TvZ, getting a Starport for Science Vessels is essential to counter Lurkers and use Irradiate against Mutalisks and Overlords. Drop play with Tanks or Marines in Dropships is a cornerstone of TvP and TvZ aggression. Wraith openings see occasional use in TvT for cloaked harass.

The Starport can lift off and relocate, and it frequently swaps add-ons with the Factory — landing on an already-built Machine Shop or vice versa to access different research. Placement should account for add-on space on the right side. Starports are typically positioned safely inside the main base since losing one disrupts critical Science Vessel or Dropship production timings.
