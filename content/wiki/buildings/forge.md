The Forge is Protoss's ground upgrade building, researching Ground Weapons, Ground Armor, and Plasma Shields (each up to level 3). It is also the prerequisite for Photon Cannon construction, linking it to both offensive cannon rushes and defensive base protection. The Forge does not produce units and has no add-ons.

In competitive play, the Forge is most iconic in PvZ where the Forge Fast Expand (FFE) is the standard opening. In FFE, the Forge is built before the Gateway to enable early Photon Cannon placement at the natural expansion, providing defense against Zergling attacks while the Protoss player secures their economy. Upgrade timing from the Forge is critical — +1 Ground Weapons significantly increases Zealot and Dragoon damage output. Double Forge builds allow simultaneous weapon and armor upgrades for faster progression to 3/3, which is a common mid-game transition.

The Forge must be placed within Pylon power. In FFE builds, the Forge is placed at the natural expansion wall-off position, often forming part of the wall alongside Gateways and Photon Cannons. In PvP and PvT, the Forge is typically built later in the game when the Protoss player is ready to invest in upgrades. Losing a Forge while an expensive upgrade is in progress is costly, so it should be protected within the base.
