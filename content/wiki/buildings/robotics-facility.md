The Robotics Facility is the Protoss production building for mechanical units: Shuttles, Reavers, and Observers. It opens up one of the most powerful and skill-intensive strategies in Brood War — the Reaver drop — by providing both the Reaver (devastating splash damage) and the Shuttle (transport) from a single building. Observers produced here are Protoss's only mobile detection unit, essential for spotting cloaked and burrowed threats.

In competitive play, the Robotics Facility represents a key tech path decision. In PvT, Reaver play is a cornerstone strategy where Shuttle-Reaver drops harass the Terran mineral line and force defensive reactions. In PvZ, early Observer production is crucial for detecting burrowed Lurkers. The Robotics Support Bay add-on building increases Reaver Scarab capacity and researches Shuttle speed, both of which dramatically improve Reaver drop effectiveness. The choice between Robotics Facility and Stargate often defines the Protoss player's mid-game approach.

The Robotics Facility must be placed within Pylon power and requires a Cybernetics Core. Placement is typically inside the main base where it is safe from harass. Losing the Robotics Facility disrupts Observer production and cuts off the Reaver drop strategy, so it should be well-protected. In some builds, a second Robotics Facility is added for faster Reaver production when committing heavily to Reaver-based play.
