The Cybernetics Core is a critical Protoss tech building that unlocks Dragoon production at the Gateway and serves as the gateway to all advanced Protoss technology. It researches Singularity Charge, which extends Dragoon attack range and is one of the first essential upgrades in any Protoss build. The Cybernetics Core also researches Air Weapons and Air Armor upgrades for Corsairs, Scouts, Carriers, and other air units.

In competitive play, the Cybernetics Core timing is one of the most important benchmarks in Protoss development. It is required before building the Stargate, Citadel of Adun, or Robotics Facility, making it the single chokepoint for all Protoss tech paths. Getting the Core quickly enables faster Dragoon production, which is essential for defense and pressure in PvT and PvP. Singularity Charge research should begin as soon as the Core completes — Dragoons without range are significantly weaker in engagements.

The Cybernetics Core must be placed within Pylon power. Since it is built early and only one is needed, placement is straightforward — typically inside the main base near the Gateway. Scouting an opponent's Cybernetics Core timing reveals their tech path: an early Core signals Dragoon-based play or fast tech, while a delayed Core suggests Gateway-heavy aggression with Zealots. Losing the Cybernetics Core before Singularity Charge completes is a serious setback to Protoss mid-game capability.
