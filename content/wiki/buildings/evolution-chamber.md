The Evolution Chamber is Zerg's primary ground upgrade building, researching Melee Attacks (for Zerglings, Ultralisks), Missile Attacks (for Hydralisks, Lurkers), and Carapace upgrades (for all ground units), each up to level 3. It is also required to morph Creep Colonies into Spore Colonies for anti-air defense and detection. The Evolution Chamber is the Zerg equivalent of Terran's Engineering Bay or Protoss's Forge.

In competitive play, the Evolution Chamber timing and upgrade choices depend heavily on the matchup and army composition. In ZvZ and ZvT, +1 Carapace is often prioritized early to increase Zergling survivability — Zerglings with +1 Carapace survive one additional hit from Marines, which is a critical breakpoint. In ZvP, +1 Missile Attacks is important for Hydralisk damage output against Dragoons. Double Evolution Chamber builds allow simultaneous attack and carapace upgrades for faster progression to 3/3, which is essential for late-game army strength.

The Evolution Chamber must be built on creep. It consumes a Drone during construction like all Zerg buildings. Placement is typically at the main base near other structures. Since it has no special strategic placement requirements, the Evolution Chamber is usually built where creep and space are available. Protecting it during expensive upgrade research is important — losing an Evolution Chamber mid-upgrade wastes both the building cost and the research investment, setting back Zerg's upgrade timing significantly.
