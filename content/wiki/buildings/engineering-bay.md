The Engineering Bay is Terran's infantry upgrade building, researching Infantry Weapons and Infantry Armor upgrades (up to level 3 each, with an Armory required for levels 2 and 3). It is also the prerequisite for constructing Missile Turrets, providing Terran with static anti-air defense and detection capability.

In competitive play, the Engineering Bay is built early in TvZ bio builds to begin +1 Infantry Weapons research, which significantly increases Marine damage output. The +1 attack timing push is a well-known strategic milestone. In TvP, upgrades from the Engineering Bay keep bio armies competitive against increasingly armored Protoss units. Double Engineering Bay builds allow simultaneous weapon and armor upgrades for faster progression to 3/3.

The Engineering Bay can lift off and relocate, though this is rarely relevant. It is frequently used as part of wall-offs at the natural ramp in TvZ, positioned alongside Supply Depots and the Barracks to form a complete wall against Zergling run-bys. Placement in the wall should ensure no gaps while leaving room for units to pass through when needed. The Engineering Bay can also be placed at expansion bases where Missile Turrets are needed for detection against burrowed or cloaked units.
