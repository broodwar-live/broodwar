The Templar Archives is the key late-game Protoss tech building, enabling High Templar and Dark Templar production at the Gateway. It researches some of the most powerful abilities in Brood War: Psionic Storm (devastating AoE damage), Hallucination (creates illusory copies of units), Mind Control (permanently takes control of enemy units via Dark Archon), and Maelstrom (freezes biological units via Dark Archon).

In competitive play, the Templar Archives unlocks Protoss's most impactful late-game tools. Psionic Storm is arguably the single most powerful ability in Brood War, capable of decimating bio armies in TvP and wiping out clustered Hydralisks in PvZ. High Templar are essential in virtually every Protoss late-game composition. Dark Templar provide a powerful harassment option — permanently cloaked units that can devastate undetected enemy worker lines. The DT rush is a well-known build that aims to end the game before the opponent has detection.

The Templar Archives requires a Citadel of Adun and must be placed within Pylon power. It is typically built safely inside the main base. Construction timing is a critical scouting indicator — early Templar Archives signals Dark Templar rush or fast storm timing. Losing the Templar Archives before key research completes can be devastating, as it delays access to Psionic Storm which defines Protoss's late-game army strength. Only one Templar Archives is ever needed since all its researches are one-time upgrades.
