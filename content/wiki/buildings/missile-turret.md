The Missile Turret is Terran's static anti-air defense structure and detector. It attacks air units with 20 explosive damage and reveals cloaked and burrowed units within its detection radius. Costing only 75 minerals with no gas requirement, the Missile Turret is an affordable solution for air defense and map detection coverage. An Engineering Bay must be built before Missile Turrets can be constructed.

In competitive play, Missile Turrets are essential for defending against Mutalisk harass in TvZ, where clusters of turrets at mineral lines and key positions force Mutalisks to engage less efficiently. They are also critical for detecting Dark Templar in TvP and spotting Lurkers in TvZ. In TvT, turrets protect against Wraith cloaking and Dropship approaches. Building a ring of turrets around the main and natural is standard practice once air threats become a concern.

Missile Turrets cannot attack ground units, so they should be complemented by other defensive structures or units. Placement at mineral lines protects workers from Mutalisk fly-bys, while turrets at the perimeter of the base provide early warning against incoming air attacks. SCVs can repair Missile Turrets during engagements to extend their survivability. With only 200 HP and 0 armor, individual turrets are fragile — their strength lies in overlapping coverage and supporting fire from nearby units.
