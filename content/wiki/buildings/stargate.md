The Stargate is the Protoss air unit production building, producing Corsairs, Scouts, Carriers (requiring a Fleet Beacon), and Arbiters (requiring an Arbiter Tribunal). It requires a Cybernetics Core and represents one of the two main Protoss tech path choices alongside the Robotics Facility. The Fleet Beacon add-on building unlocks Carrier production and advanced air upgrades, while the Arbiter Tribunal enables Arbiter production with Recall and Stasis Field.

In competitive play, the Stargate is most prominent in PvZ, where Corsair production is a core strategy for clearing Overlords (denying Zerg scouting and supply) and supporting ground armies against Mutalisks. Corsair-Reaver and Corsair-Dark Templar are well-known PvZ compositions that begin with Stargate openings. Carrier builds are less common in professional play but remain a viable late-game option. Arbiters provide Recall for tactical teleportation of armies and cloaking field for nearby units, seeing use in PvT and PvZ late game.

The Stargate must be placed within Pylon power and requires a Cybernetics Core. It is typically placed safely inside the main base. The choice to open Stargate versus Robotics Facility is a defining strategic decision that shapes the entire mid-game. Scouting an opponent's Stargate alerts the other player to prepare for Corsairs, potential Carrier transitions, or Arbiter-based strategies. Protecting the Stargate is important, as losing it disrupts Corsair production flow and delays air-based tech paths.
