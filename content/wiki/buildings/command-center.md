The Command Center is Terran's main base structure, responsible for producing SCVs and serving as the economic heart of every base. It is the first building a Terran player starts with and the foundation upon which all Terran tech is built. The Command Center supports two mutually exclusive add-ons: the Comsat Station, which provides Scanner Sweep for instant detection and scouting anywhere on the map, and the Nuclear Silo, which manufactures nuclear missiles for Ghost strikes.

In competitive play, the Command Center is central to expansion timings. Players commonly construct a second Command Center inside the main base and then float it to the natural expansion once it is safe — a technique that protects the investment from early aggression. Fast CC builds sacrifice early military pressure for a stronger economy, while delayed expansions prioritize aggression or tech. The Comsat Station add-on is almost always preferred in professional games for its detection and map awareness capabilities.

The Command Center can lift off and relocate, making it unique among main base structures. This allows Terran players to dodge attacks, reposition to new mining locations, or escape base trades. Placement is flexible since it does not require add-on space until a Comsat Station or Nuclear Silo is attached. With 1500 HP and 1 armor, the Command Center is one of the most durable buildings in the game.
