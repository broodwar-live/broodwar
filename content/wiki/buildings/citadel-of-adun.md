The Citadel of Adun is a Protoss tech building that researches Leg Enhancements, giving Zealots a significant movement speed boost that transforms them from slow frontline units into fast, aggressive melee fighters. The Citadel is also the prerequisite for the Templar Archives, making it a mandatory step on the path to High Templar, Dark Templar, and Psionic Storm.

In competitive play, the Citadel of Adun is a key timing building. In PvZ, Zealot speed (Leg Enhancements) is essential for Zealots to catch and surround Zerg units effectively. Fast Citadel builds enable early speed Zealot pressure that can disrupt Zerg economy. In PvP, getting Zealot speed before the opponent provides a significant advantage in Zealot-vs-Zealot engagements. The Citadel is also the gateway to storm tech — players who want fast Psionic Storm must prioritize Citadel construction to begin the tech chain toward Templar Archives as quickly as possible.

The Citadel of Adun must be placed within Pylon power and requires a Cybernetics Core. It is typically built inside the main base without special placement concerns. Since it serves as both a research building (Leg Enhancements) and a tech prerequisite (Templar Archives), its construction timing signals important strategic information to opponents — an early Citadel suggests either fast Zealot speed aggression or a rush toward storm technology.
